📋 Reminders & Rules

📌 Reminders
  • No matter how often you have visited, always follow every applicable checklist step — things at the Cabin change.
  • Each instruction is critical. Failure to follow and document steps may result in financial consequences or loss of Cabin privileges.
  • Keys to the Bunkhouse and Garage are on a key fob by the back entrance door. This key fob must stay at the Cabin. Replacement cost: $75.
  • The Riding Lawnmower key fob must stay at the Cabin. Replacement cost: $75.
  • Report all damages immediately with photos to Dave Johnson (801-599-9634) and Russ Margetts (801-891-3100).
  • Use PINK RV ANTIFREEZE ONLY in drains and toilet during winter closing.
📜 Rules
  • The individual the reservation is in the name of is responsible for all individuals in their party — ensuring adults and children understand and follow the rules.
  • The reservation holder is responsible to explicitly follow and document the RusLyn Pines Cabin Opening and Closing Checklist.
  • Respect the Cabin, Bunkhouse, Garage and property. Respect the furnishings; ensure all in your party do the same — especially children who may not know better.
  • All Cabin stays must be "no trace." Don't let the next group be able to tell you were there. Leave the Cabin, Bunkhouse and Garage better than you found it.
  • All guests must obey all Diamond Dell HOA rules.
  • All guests must be aware of and adhere to US Forest Service laws and regulations (e.g., open fires).
  • Promptly report any problems to Russ (801-891-3100) and Dave (801-599-9634). In emergencies, call 911.
  • No alcohol of any kind is allowed on RusLyn Pines property.
  • No smoking of any product is allowed on RusLyn Pines property.
  • Illegal drugs of any kind are not allowed on RusLyn Pines property.
  • Firearms must be properly secured on RusLyn Pines property.
  • No open fires (e.g., candles) are allowed inside the Cabin, Bunkhouse or Garage.
  • Open fire is only permitted in the designated fire pit; no fire in the firepit unless permission is granted by a RusLyn Pines Board member.
  • No large bonfires, regardless of US Forest Service allowances, on RusLyn Pines property.
  • When the firepit is used, a water hose must be adjacent and available for fire safety.
  • No fireworks of any kind are allowed on RusLyn Pines property.
  • Changes to the RusLyn Pines technology environment (e.g., Wi-Fi, HDMI inputs, cameras, etc.) in the Cabin, Bunkhouse or property is not allowed.
  • No riding off-road vehicles around the Cabin on RusLyn Pines property; such vehicles must be used outside of the HOA property.
  • Respect the privacy of all other Cabin owners and their property.
  • The Cabin owners down the dike to the right (beyond the locked gate) are a separate association and do not allow trespassing beyond the gate. They have cameras and will prosecute violators.
  • Do not remove items that belong to the Cabin. Exception: if bedding or rugs become soiled, take them home to be cleaned and promptly return them to Sharon, Diane, Marianne or Russ.
  • Take home everything you bring to the Cabin, including — especially — garbage.
  • In summer, everyone who stays must mow the grass in the meadow, around the Cabin, Bunkhouse and Garage.
  • Before donating anything to the Cabin, you must first have permission from the RusLyn Pines board.
  • Rule of thumb: when in doubt, get permission from the RusLyn Pines board first. Permission always precludes and trumps forgiveness!
  • Ignorance of any of the above rules is not an excuse.
  • Failure to comply with all of these rules may result in the loss of privilege to use RusLyn Pines.
