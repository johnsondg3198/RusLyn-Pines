📋 Cabin Rules

  • No matter how often you have visited, always follow every applicable checklist step — things at the Cabin change.
  • Each instruction is critical. Failure to follow and document steps may result in financial consequences or loss of Cabin privileges.
  • Keys to the Bunkhouse and Garage are on a key fob by the back entrance door. This key fob must stay at the Cabin. Replacement cost: $75.
  • The Riding Lawnmower key fob must stay at the Cabin. Replacement cost: $75.
  • Do not leave food on any surface — rodents are quickly attracted to accessible food.
  • Haul all garbage home with you — do not leave it in the Garage.
  • Campfires must be fully extinguished before sleeping or leaving.
  • Do not cut live trees or damage vegetation on the property.
  • Report all damages immediately with photos to Dave Johnson (801-599-9634) and Russ Margetts (801-891-3100).
  • Use PINK RV ANTIFREEZE ONLY in drains and toilet during winter closing.
